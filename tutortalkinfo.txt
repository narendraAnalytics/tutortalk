DESCRIPTION
-----------
TutorTalk is an AI-powered voice learning platform that puts a personal tutor
and exam conductor in every student's pocket. Using Google's Gemini Live API,
it conducts real-time spoken conversations — guiding students through concepts
via the Socratic method, and testing them with voice-based MCQ exams. All
sessions are transcribed live and available as downloadable PDF reports.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

CORE FEATURES
-------------

Feature 1 : 
[ TRAIN MODE — Socratic Voice Tutor ]
  • Fully voice-driven AI tutor powered by Gemini 3.1 Flash Live
  • Never gives answers directly — guides students to discover answers through
    targeted questions (Socratic method)
  • Choose Study Level: Class 10 / Intermediate / JEE / NEET / UPSC
  • Choose Subject, Topic (optional), and Difficulty (Easy / Medium / Hard)
  • Live real-time transcription — both student and AI speech shown as chat
  • Animated VoiceOrb visualizes AI state: Idle → Listening → Speaking → Interrupted
  • Session auto-ends at plan time limit (5 min free / 30 min Plus)
  • Downloadable PDF transcript after every session.


Feature 2 : 
[ EXAM MODE — Voice MCQ Exam Conductor ]
  • AI reads questions aloud; student answers by voice
  • Instant spoken feedback: "Correct!" or "Incorrect" + brief explanation
  • Competitive exam scoring: +4 correct / −1 wrong
  • Choose Level, Subject, and Number of Questions (up to 5 free / unlimited paid)
  • Countdown timer: 1 minute per question — auto-ends when time runs out
  • Live score panel: current marks, questions answered, accuracy %
  • Results screen: final marks, max marks, accuracy, time taken
  • 5 exam levels with pattern-specific AI prompts: Class 10, Intermediate,
    JEE, NEET, UPSC

Feature 3 :   
[ DASHBOARD ]
  • View all past Train and Exam sessions
  • Session cards show subject, duration, type badge, and exam score
  • Aggregate metrics: total sessions, total minutes, topics covered
  • Quota tracker: sessions left and exams left (free plan)
  • History limited to 1 session for free users; unlimited for Plus/Pro

Feature 4 : 
[ SMART PLAN GATING ]
  • Subjects, difficulty levels, question counts, and session limits all gated
    by subscription plan
  • Visual locks on unavailable options — no hidden paywalls
  • Plan badge shown in navbar at all times (Free / Plus / Pro)

REAL-WORLD PROBLEMS SOLVED
--------------------------

  1. PASSIVE LEARNING
     Most students read and re-read without truly understanding. TutorTalk's
     Socratic tutor forces active recall and reasoning — the AI never hands
     over an answer, making students think it through step by step.

  2. NO IMMEDIATE EXAM FEEDBACK
     Traditional practice papers give feedback days later — or never. TutorTalk's
     exam mode delivers instant spoken feedback after every answer, with a brief
     explanation, so students learn in the moment.

  3. EXAM ANXIETY & UNFAMILIARITY WITH PATTERNS
     Competitive exams like JEE, NEET, and UPSC have specific question styles
     that students rarely encounter in regular school. TutorTalk uses level-specific
     AI prompts that mirror real exam patterns, building familiarity and confidence.

  4. EXPENSIVE / INACCESSIBLE TUTORING
     Quality tutors cost hundreds per hour and are geographically limited.
     TutorTalk delivers an always-available, patient AI voice tutor at a fraction
     of the cost — free tier included.

  5. NO STUDY ACCOUNTABILITY
     Students forget what they studied and can't track their own growth.
     Session transcripts, PDF downloads, and a dashboard with full history
     create a personal academic record they can review anytime.

  6. FRAGMENTED STUDY TOOLS
     Students juggle different apps for tutoring, exam practice, and progress
     tracking. TutorTalk unifies all three in one voice-first platform with a
     single login and shared session history.
